The Story Begins

Book I

A world in which two powers coexist: the Empire of Embla, which yearns to subdue the heroes of other worlds, and the Kingdom of Askr, which opposes its plans. Your avatar possesses great summoning powers that allow it to summon legendary heroes from different Fire Emblem worlds. Join the Guardians of Askr to save their kingdom from destruction!

Book II

Nifl, the kingdom of ice, and Muspell, the kingdom of fire. These two nations were at odds until Muspell's troops, led by Surtr, King Igneous, overwhelmed Nifl and brought the conflict to a bitter end. Fjorm, the princess of Nifl, escaped the destruction of her home and met the Guardians of Askr. Fjorm hopes to restore his kingdom with the help of the Guardians, but Muspell's army has also reached Askr...

Book III

The fierce battle of Muspell has come to an end and the Guardians have returned to Askr. However, shortly after their return, they are attacked by the soldiers of Hel, the realm of the dead. While fighting to try to drive out Hel's army, Alfonse and the others come across some dead generals who happen to be historical figures, warriors claiming to be the greatest kings in history, LÃ­f and Thrasir. At the same time, Hel, the ruler of the realm of the dead, gives a chilling order to her daughter, Princess Eir, regarding the Guardians of Askr: "Join their army, obey them and give yourself completely to their cause. And when you have earned their trust, I will give you the order to kill them. " So Eir joins the Guardians, but her eyes reveal that her mother's order grieves her greatly.

Book IV

The battle against the cohort of the dead has come to an end. Now, the Guardians set out to investigate the strange rumors that come to them from a distant village, where they say that its residents have fallen prey to a deep lethargy plagued by terrible nightmares. Upon reaching the vicinity of the village, Alfonse and the rest of the Guardians are invaded by a sudden drowsiness that plunges them into a semi-conscious state. It is then that Peony appears, a radiant elf with the power to conjure pleasant dreams. Hand in hand with this new ally, the Guardians of Askr will have to fight to escape from their dream prison and from the dark elves that will stand in the way of their return to reality.
